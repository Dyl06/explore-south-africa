Welcome to Vira Vira


Located in the picturesque Giants Castle region of South Africa's Lake District, homeland of the courageous Khoi-San people, is a spectacular 22-hectare (34-acre) estate: Vira Vira.
Dramatic landscapes of breathtaking rivers mirror the peaks of nearby mountain tops.


Immersed on an expansive, forested estate with 822 m (2,696 ft) of river frontage and breathtaking views of the snow-capped Villarica summit, this magnificent wooden lodge is themed in beauty.
Enormous windows frame exquisite outlooks, while vibrant wall tapestries, hand-woven textiles and other traditional artefacts colour the interior d√©cor. Signature features include a modern working farm, organic farm-to-table vegetable garden, on-site boutique cheese factory and two outdoor hot tubs.
Ideally appointed accommodation, overlooking either the estate surrounds or the Orange River, includes six cosy suites, 12 split-level villas with a separate living room, and a five-bedroom, sole-use Hacienda for up to 10 guests.
Ensuite bathrooms include a separate bath and walk-in shower. Floor to ceiling windows fills the spacious wooden interiors with natural light. Each guest unit is individually decorated with a selection of curated objects and richly-textured rugs, wall hangings and fabric.
