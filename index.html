Destination

Safarii

Experience some of the best wildlife and diversity South Africa has to offer from the comfort of our open top safari 4x4's. Retire in the evenings next to the camp fire while you watch the sun set over the African savanna with an iconic G&T in hand.

Mountain

Magnificient landscapes towering above as you explore the oldest mountain range in Africa, the Drakensberg Mountain. Wether its the sleeping giant or the stream gentling bubbling past you there is something for everyone here.

Beach

Fall asleep with the sound of the Indian Ocean waves rolling onto white sandy beaches. Take a refreshing dip in the morning before heading into the shade where breakfast awaits. Experience the magnificient colors while snorkelling in the rock pools or go for long strolls along the private beach as you watch the sun setting over the ocean.
