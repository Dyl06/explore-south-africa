Destinations

Safari

Experience some of the best wildlife and diversity South Africa has to offer from the comfort of our open top safari 4x4's. Retire in the evenings next to the camp fire while you watch the sun set over the African savanna with an iconic G&T in hand.

Beach

Fall asleep with the sound of the Indian Ocean waves rolling onto white sandy beaches. Take a refreshing dip in the morning before heading into the shade where breakfast awaits. Experience the magnificient colors while snorkelling in the rock pools or go for long strolls along the private beach as you watch the sun setting over the ocean.

Mountain

Magnificient landscapes towering above as you explore the oldest mountain range in Africa, the Drakensberg Mountains. Wether its the sleeping giant or the stream gently bubbling past you there is something for everyone here.

About Us

Our luxury lodges and camps are perfectly situated to showcase the very best of South Africa's Safari, Beach and Mountain destinations. Each has a unique style and character that blend into the awe-inspiring natural surroundings. Our accomodation offers an authentic African experience, complemented by welcoming local staff and expert guides.
